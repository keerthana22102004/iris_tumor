Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How does the Iris Tumor Detection system work?

The system uses machine learning models, particularly convolutional neural networks (CNNs), to analyze images of the iris. These models are trained to recognize patterns that indicate the presence of tumors. Once an image is uploaded, the system processes it and provides a result indicating the likelihood of a tumor.

2. Can I upload any image of the iris for detection?

To ensure the best results, it's recommended to upload high-quality, clear images of the iris. Low-resolution or blurry images may reduce the accuracy of detection.

3. What should I do if the system detects a tumor?

If the system indicates the possibility of a tumor, it is essential to seek a professional medical evaluation. The system can only provide an initial analysis, and a healthcare professional should confirm the diagnosis and guide you on the next steps for treatment or further investigation.

4. Is the Iris Tumor Detection system free to use?

Yes, it is absolutely free.
